Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility requirements? +
You need GNM or ANM registration, a valid nursing license, minimum 2 years of clinical experience, a smartphone with internet connection, and willingness to provide home healthcare services in your area.
How soon can I start receiving visit requests? +
After verification (usually within 24 hours) and successful completion of your interview, you can start receiving requests. Most nurses get their first booking within 48 hours of activation.
How much will I earn per visit? +
‚Çπ600 per home visit with same-day payout directly to your bank account. No hidden charges or commissions. You keep 100% of what you earn.
Can I choose my own working hours? +
Yes, absolutely! You have complete control over your schedule. Set your availability in the app and accept or decline visit requests as you wish. Work full-time, part-time, or only on weekends.
Are there any registration fees or subscriptions? +
No fees, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Joining our platform is completely free. Start earning without any upfront investment.
What types of services will I provide? +
You'll provide patient care monitoring, administer medications and injections, perform clinical procedures like cannulation and catheter care, wound dressing, and maintain patient documentation.
How far will I need to travel for visits? +
You only receive visit requests from your nearby area based on your location preferences. This minimizes travel time and costs, ensuring maximum convenience.
Is patient information provided before the visit? +
Yes! You receive complete patient information, required services, and visit details in the app before accepting any request. All patients are pre-verified for your safety.
